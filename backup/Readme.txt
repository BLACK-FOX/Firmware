##########################################################################################
													Upload firmware steps
##########################################################################################
IMPORTANT: 
Please distinguish the type of control panel, ZRIB contol board is Blue or Red, 
and ZRIB control board is Black
==========================================================================================
													For ZRIB control board
==========================================================================================
Windows:
==========================================================================================
1. Download firmware upgrade tools (\HEX\Firmware Upload tools.zip)
Download link: https://drive.google.com/open?id=0B9Z1DbrxfqbpT1l1STRmbGdKaE0
2. Download HEX file form directory: (\HEX\[your 3d printer model]\)
3. Unzip "Firmware Upload tools.zip" , there is a user guide in this zip file, please read it. 
4. Connect the printer to your PC, please make sure driver has been installed correctly. 
Connect printer to PC by USB cable, Check in "Device Manager" of your PC-->Port(Com & LPT)-->Silicon Labs Cp210x USD to UART Bridge(COMx). 
Dirver download link: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/0B9Z1DbrxfqbpYWRaWVZGT3dBWTQ
5. Follow "AVRUpdateTools userGuide_ZRIB.pdf" to upload the firmware to your control board.
==========================================================================================
MacOS:
==========================================================================================
1. Download Cura from the below link: https://ultimaker.com/en/products/ultimaker-cura-software
2. Follow "AVRUpdateTools userGuide_Cura.pdf" to upload the firmware to your control board.
==========================================================================================
